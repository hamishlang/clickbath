Click Bath

By Hamish Lang

What is this?
Click Bath is an audio sauna. Simply click around, fiddle with some knobs, and make some noises!

How do I use it?
For best results, please use on desktop with wired, stereo headphones.
If you use Bluetooth or other wireless headphones this may cause delays or issues with the audio playback.

Selecting Options will give you access to the tones for each 'pad', and the scale determines the notes that are played.

If you're not familiar with musical scales, you can simply select a mood, or alternatively, try out one of the presets within the separate Presets section.

You can additionally use your current location to select a tone and scale based on your weather. When selecting this, you will be prompted to allow the browser to get your location, make sure to say yes. This works on mobile, though it can be a little janky depending on the device and browser. If you're not hearing anything make sure you're not in silent mode, and again, ensure that you're connected to your device with cabled headphones (Bluetooth or wireless headphones will sound not so great).

How was this made?
With good old Javascript, Jquery and a huge amount of Tone.js (thanks!).
